## Common Questions

### Why is the app so small (under 5 MB)?
Every sound is generated live by code, not played back from audio files. Nothing to download, nothing to stream.

### Does it work offline?
Yes. Murmur makes zero network requests. Turn on airplane mode and it works exactly the same.

### Where does my data go?
Nowhere. All stats, favorites, and mixes stay on your device. We have no servers, no cloud sync (yet), and no analytics.

### The sleep timer stopped playback — is this a bug?
No, that's the feature. Murmur fades audio to silence over the duration you set. Tap the Timer tab to adjust.

### Can I request a new sound or preset?
Absolutely. Email me. If 3+ people request the same thing, I'll prioritize it for the next update.

### The lock-screen controls don't work
Make sure Background App Refresh is enabled for Murmur (Settings → General → Background App Refresh → Murmur). If that's on and it still doesn't work, email me with your iPhone model and iOS version.

### I paid for an in-app purchase but nothing unlocked
There are no in-app purchases in Murmur 1.0. If you see a paywall, that's a fake — do not enter payment info, and please report it to me immediately.

### How do I reset all my statistics?
Settings → Data → Reset all statistics.

### Does Murmur work on iPad?
Yes, but it's designed for iPhone. The iPad version runs in compatibility mode.
