# Privacy Policy for Murmur

**Last updated:** 2026-04-17
**Developer:** xiaokang.lv
**Contact:** 2089882154@qq.com

## TL;DR

Murmur **does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data**. All data stays on your device. We have no servers. We have no analytics. We have no ads. You owe us nothing.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**None.**

Murmur runs entirely on your device. The app contains no account system, no login, no cloud sync, no analytics SDK, and no advertising frameworks.

## 2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Murmur stores the following data **only on your device**, using the standard iOS `UserDefaults` system. This data never leaves your device:

- Your last sound mix (which sounds were active and at what volume)
- Your favorite sounds
- Your usage statistics (daily minutes, streaks, most-used sounds) — displayed in the Stats tab
- Your breathing / timer preferences

You can delete all of this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

## 3. Permissions

Murmur requests the following system permissions:

- **Background Audio**: So that sounds continue playing when the screen is locked. Sounds are generated in real time on your device; no audio data is transmitted.

Murmur does **not** request: microphone, camera, location, contacts, calendar, photos, health, or any network-based permission.

## 4. Network Activity

Murmur **does not connect to the internet**. If you see your device reporting network activity while Murmur is running, it is from iOS system processes, not from Murmur.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Murmur does **not** integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## 6. Data Sharing

We have no data to share. Even if we wanted to, there is nothing to sell.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Murmur does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes (e.g., if a future version adds optional iCloud sync), we will update this page and notify you in-app before any change takes effect.
